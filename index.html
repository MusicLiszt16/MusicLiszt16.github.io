about basilean

Basilean is a society at Bob Jones University dedicated to helping young men grow to be Men of the Kingdom.

Following the acronym of B.A.S.I.L. (Brotherhood, Authenticity, Service, Intentionality, Legacy), Basilean glorifies God through a variety of music, sports, outreaches, and more.

To find out more about Basilean as a society in whole, please visit www.basileaneagles.com to find out more.
